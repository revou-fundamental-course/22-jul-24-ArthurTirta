Our Package

Bali

An Indonesian island, Bali is a tropical paradise known for its lush rice terraces, stunning beaches, and vibrant Hindu culture. Visitors can explore ancient temples, enjoy water sports, and indulge in Balinese cuisine. Ubud, with its art scene and monkey forest, is a must-visit.

Bromo

Located in East Java, Indonesia, Mount Bromo is an active volcano surrounded by a vast, otherworldly landscape called the Sea of Sand. Visitors can hike to its crater rim for sunrise views and witness the smoking caldera. It’s a surreal experience!

Toraja

In South Sulawesi, Indonesia, Tana Toraja is known for its unique culture and elaborate funeral ceremonies. The Toraja people have distinctive architecture, traditional houses called “tongkonan,” and intricate wood carvings. The region’s lush hills and rice terraces add to its charm.

Italy

Known for its rich history, art, and cuisine, Italy is a country in southern Europe. It’s home to iconic landmarks like the Colosseum in Rome, the Leaning Tower of Pisa, and the canals of Venice. Italy is famous for its pasta, pizza, and gelato, as well as its beautiful coastlines along the Amalfi Coast and Cinque Terre.

Morroco

Located in North Africa, Morocco is a vibrant country with a mix of Arab, Berber, and European influences. Its bustling markets (souks), intricate architecture (such as in Marrakech), and the Sahara Desert are major attractions. Don’t miss out on trying traditional Moroccan mint tea!

United States

A diverse and vast country in North America, the United States is known for its cultural melting pot, natural wonders, and iconic cities. From the skyscrapers of New York City to the Grand Canyon in Arizona, there’s something for everyone. The U.S. is also famous for Hollywood, jazz music, and its national parks.
